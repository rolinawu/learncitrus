What is Citrus?

Citrus is a social learning platform for students and educators. At Citrus, we believe in embracing the social, emotional, and personal aspects of learning.

Teachers: Educating the world's children

Are you a teacher known for your hilarious jokes and engaging lectures? Why not share your talent and change the lives of millions of students around the world? Join us in delivering high-quality education to students across cultures and social classes. Contact us to find out more about how to upload your content.

Students: Study with friends and make new ones

Friends who learn together stay together. Meet new friends or work with existing ones, form study groups and share notes. We perform best when we work together. Chat, laugh, and inspire. Sign up for an exclusive invite!
